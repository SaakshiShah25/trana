About

During the difficult situation around the world when the medical resources are mainly directed to the pandemic, trana.live aims at helping the people stuck at homes who’re in need of medication, clinical help or wanting to request help, but because they are non-covid19 and are non-emergency, they are given less priority.

How it works

Once registered a user can easily enter the medical issues or request the medication on the platform. When we receive the respective request, get a consultation from the medical authorities regarding the same, & post the medicine request which is visible to all the registered pharmacy stores in the geo fenced area of 10 km around the user’s house. As the requests are addressed a notification is generated to the user via an email.

On the part of the pharmacist, once the required prescription being uploaded on the website the respective pharmacies that lie in the range of the 10kms from the problem being raised get a notification, his work is then to look for the availability of those medicines and update about the same. Once it’s checked that there’s the availability of the same prescribed medicine the user gets to purchase it to the told shop rather than wandering to a number of them.

In case a patient needs help beyond the need of medication he/she just have to report the health from the respective logged in account, the authority then looks for the necessary solution like acknowledging the issues, providing medical consultation or precautionary need for an ambulance.

All that’s needed in this effort that is solved by the trana.live solution to help you, we need your help in return. We will provide help readily once you’ve made us aware about your need of help.
