User Manual

Below is the description of how the solution serves you

A mobile web platform which can be accessed from both mobile device and computers.

This is how the homepage appears.

Next, you click on get started in order to request help you will see the following form:

The Sign Up screen

Once you’re done putting your entries in, you will then see three options as under user, authority and pharmacist.

  1. If you’re a user, that is a patient who needs to request for medications or clinical help; click on the user and you’re good to get registered.
  2. If you’re an authority then you will be verified by our team personally and then the login credentials will be allowed.
  3. If you are a pharmacist, then choose the last option to get the medication requests from the patients or the users.

After you’ve logged in as a user you can enter how you seek help by choosing the respective options.

Once you’ve chosen the means of help, you then have to fill a few details regarding the same.

How does the User side work?

The user enters what kind of help is needed, the medicine prescription or name of the medicine and the clinical details. Those details reach our server and then it reaches the registered pharmacy stores or registered medical authority within 10kms area of the user.

Report Condition & Request for Medical consultation from Authority

Request for medicines

The authority or pharmacy shops then responds to the request respectively and the user receives an email notification with message and details of the Pharmacy store in case of request for medicine or acknowledgement of the consultation with authority.

What Pharmacists see?

The user’s medicine request is visible to all the registered pharmacists in the vicinity of 10 km of the users. Any pharmacy who has the availability of the medicine can respond and the user gets an email notification.

On the home screen of your accessed page you will be able to see the details about the medication needed. If you can make those available to the user, click available so that the user can reach out to you to obtain the same.
