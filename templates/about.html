About Us

Trana.live
is an initiative of helping those who are in need of help. With the belief in helping you we’ve come about with this web solution that does not only keep a continuous check on one’s medical condition but also provides precautionary medical attention and consultation.

In a situation like world pandemic we realised that not only those who’re suffering from this fatal disease need medical attention but those who have their existing situations or those who develop some non-emergency health issues whilst all these also need equal attention.

Considering all the possible problems we, a bunch of students came across with the idea of developing a mobile web solution for providing the virtual help to those health conditions that need some attention but are deprived of the same.

It’s the integrated effort that makes help possible. We’re whole heartedly ready to help. Just HELP US HELP YOU.
