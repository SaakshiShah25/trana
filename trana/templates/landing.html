Protecting Non-Covid Patients too
Although we are taking immense care of the COVID-19 patients and avoid it's spread,
We do not want to neglect your illness, medical issues & problems. Hence we are here for you!
How we can help
In order to let us help you, we will need certain information. Tell us about your problems. How are you feeling? Tell us if you need any medicines! We will try an arrange everything for you. We understand how important it is to be heard in such times. We are here for you!
Report Conditions
Simply report your condition to the authorities. Give as much details about your condition possible. Help other people who are unable to upload details.
Order Medicines
In this lockdown, everythin is shut! Some Pharmacies might or might not have what you need! We can directly send your request to the nearby pharmacy. We'll notify once they have it.
Chat with a doctor
Cough! Cough! Feeling feverish? Feeling even more feverish thinking it might be COVID-19? Dont feel like going to the doctor? You can chat with our registered doctors who will help you to diagnose the problem.
